Evaluation Report — Track-conditioned Video-to-Video Generation

Click video to play/pause  ·  Space to pause all  ·  5 experiments  ·  50 qualitative samples × 3 augs

Quantitative Results

FVD ↓ Fréchet Video Distance Overall generation quality. Fréchet distance between generated and GT target video distributions in S3D feature space. Lower means closer to real video quality.
L2 ↓ Frame-level Pixel L2 Pixel-level similarity between generated and GT target video. Mean per-frame RGB difference averaged across all frames. Lower means closer appearance to GT.
DINO ↑ DINOv2 Cosine Similarity Semantic similarity between generated and source video. Frame-averaged cosine similarity of DINOv2 CLS tokens. Higher means the source appearance and style are better preserved.
EPE ↓ End-Point Error (BootsTAPIR) Track-following accuracy. First-frame query points of target tracks are re-tracked in the generated video via BootsTAPIR; pixel distance to GT target tracks. Lower means the motion condition is followed more precisely.
